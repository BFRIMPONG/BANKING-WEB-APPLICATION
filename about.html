About Us

Insights into how we have become Africa's Global.
BIX Bank has been serving businesses and communities of
Africa for 120 years.

120 years of uninterrupted banking operations

Currently, BIX Bank has 19 African subsidiaries, contributing about 20% of the Group’s balance sheet, with a target of contributing 50%. We’re on a quest to build the strongest domestic and African brand.






History


BIX Bank (Ghana) Limited pioneered the entry of a new generation of foreign banks into the country in January 2005.
The presence of BIX Bank Ghana in the banking industry in Ghana over the last thirteen years has galvanized banking onto a higher performance level where competition and innovation in responding to the needs of the customer have become the driving force of the industry.






Leadership


The management team of the group is made up of a crop of seasoned and industry-recognized professionals, with diverse (but complementary) skills in various backgrounds, as well as depth of experience (garnered from national and international institutions).

Our Mission


To be a role model for African businesses by creating superior value for all our stakeholders, abiding by the utmost professional and ethical standards, and by building an enduring institution.

Our Vision


To be the undisputed leading and dominant financial services institution in Africa.
