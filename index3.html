E-Banking

We have a range of retail e-products that allows you
to securely and conveniently access your bank account
information and do banking transactions via our website
and your mobile phone.

Mobile Banking

UBA Mobile Banking App is an electronic banking
product that allows customers to access their bank
account via their mobile phones.

Internet Banking

Internet banking service allows customers to conduct
financial transactions via the world wide web from their
home, office or on the move.

Notification Services


Notification services is a platform that allows customers
receive notice of transactions in your account(s).

Instant Bills


BIX Bank's online collection platform will enable you accept
payment of funds on the web via UBA payment gateway.
Instant Bills Pay is our hosted online payment bill
collection website for various billers. Each biller has a
dedicated page with customizable collection platform.
